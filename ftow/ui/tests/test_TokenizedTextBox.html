by default TokenizedTextBox only tokenizes on TAB and ENTER keys, or onBlur. there are three settings for changing this behavior: tokenizeKeys, tokenizeChars, and tokenizeRegExp. tokenizeChars is translated internally into tokenizeRegExp.
